pancakes.love

Experiments in care, play, ecology, and strange software.

pancakes.love is the experimental side of the Pancakes ecosystem: games, playlists, prototypes, ambient worlds, symbolic projections, and ideas that are still finding their shape.

Some work here may eventually become part of the stable Pancakes platform. Some of it may remain exploratory, poetic, playful, or simply useful for thinking.
