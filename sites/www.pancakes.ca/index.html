www.pancakes.ca

A public home for the Pancakes ecosystem.

Pancakes is a developing ecosystem for care, stewardship, local-first recordkeeping, and cooperative software. This site is the stable public front door, not the whole system.

What it is

A product and documentation surface for humane life computing: software that supports ordinary life without turning everything into surveillance, extraction, or frantic optimization.

What it is now

An active, source-controlled public project with a canonical documentation site at docs.pancakes.ca and an incubating privacy-first cycle-tracking project at www.redwitch.ca.

Where to go next

  • docs.pancakes.ca product documentation, governance, and architecture notes
  • pancakes.love the experimental and cultural surface
  • Red Witch private cycle tracking and reproductive data stewardship
  • Floating-Eye-Software/pancakes the pancakes source repository
  • Floating-Eye-Software/pitchfork the pitchfork source repository

Current status

Pancakes is still maturing. The public story is deliberately narrower than the internal design space: this home explains the project, points to the documentation and active projects, and leaves the stable application claims for later.
