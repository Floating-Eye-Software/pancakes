Pancakes

Humane life computing for ordinary life.

Pancakes is a developing ecosystem of software for care, rhythm, stewardship, local-first recordkeeping, cooperative systems, and humane technology.

The project begins from a simple premise: ordinary life contains forms of care, work, reflection, rest, memory, and contribution that should be understandable and supportable without becoming surveillance, productivity extraction, or behavioral wage labor.

Current top-level efforts

Pancakes

The human-facing Wellness Notebook and broader local-first life platform.

Pitchfork

A symbolic accounting, covenant, projection, node-governance, and anti-extraction policy substrate for Pancakes clients.

Red Witch

Privacy-first cycle tracking, reproductive recordkeeping, and data stewardship. Red Witch has its own dedicated public home.
